WAREHOUSE

PBX Logistics, through their warehouse in Burnaby, BC, offers temperature controlled storage capacities. Their multi-door warehouse enables the swift and efficient integration with trucking services. To facilitate understanding, to service and meet customer needs, representatives are available on a 24/7 basis.

group

DISTRIBUTION

The cross-docking facilities, ocean shipping container de-stuffing and transloading services and inventory management enable the PBX team to receive and re-route incoming shipments to multiple destinations. PBX offers truckload and less than truckload services throughout many areas of North America, particularly on the American I-5 corridor and Eastern USA and has the necessary warehouse and distribution ability to support the over the road service.

WHO ARE WE?

PBX Logistics has grown rapidly largely thanks to its committed drivers and working ownership. Only team drivers are utilized and to maintain the high standard of the driver pool, trainers are hired to ensure this standard is maintained. By concentrating on providing service to our clients to meet their just-in-time requirements through flexible and tailored solutions, PBX Logistics has grown steadily. With Jason, Miljan and Shamim working full time, PBX has been able to sustain a high level of customer satisfaction.
