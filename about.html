About us

PBX Logistics has grown rapidly largely thanks to its committed drivers and working ownership. Only team drivers are utilized and to maintain the high standard of the driver pool, trainers are hired to ensure this standard is maintained.

By concentrating on providing service to our clients to meet their just-in-time requirements through flexible and tailored solutions, PBX Logistics has grown steadily. With Jason, Miljan and Shamim working full time, PBX has been able to sustain a high level of customer satisfaction.
